层级3：强劲

Level 3: Strong

In our experience, the majority of startups never get to this level. If you get to strong product-market fit, your company has the potential to be very valuable.

根据我们的经验，大多数初创公司从未达到这个层级。如果你达到了强劲的产品市场契合度，你的公司有可能非常有价值。

The big difference between Level 2 and Level 3 is that the demand floodgates have opened. There's even more repeatability. Demand is coming inbound. People have heard of you. The company feels like it's humming.

Level 2和Level 3之间的最大区别是需求闸门已经打开。有更多的可重复性。需求正在涌入。人们听说过你。公司感觉正在嗡嗡作响。

感觉如何

What it Feels Like

我们和某人交谈过，他说找到产品市场契合度是如此直观，你立即就能感觉到——就像间歇泉爆发一样。老实说，我们在头几年从未有过这种感觉。

We talked to someone who said that finding product-market fit was so visceral that you immediately felt it — like a geyser exploding. And we honestly never felt that in the first couple years.

在Retool，我们获得的每一个早期客户，无论是第四个还是第十四个，都感觉像是我们将要找到的最后一个客户。感觉更像是在滚石上山。

At Retool every early customer we got, whether that was number four or number 14, felt like the last customer we were ever going to find. It felt more like rolling the stone uphill.

最大的转变是获取潜在客户的容易程度。我记得当时想，我甚至不知道这些潜在客户是从哪里来的。而且每个月都有更多的潜在客户出现。

The biggest shift was in the ease of getting leads. I remember thinking, 'I don't even know where these leads are coming from.' And more just kept showing up every month.

在2018年第一年的销售之后，接下来的两年是疯狂的。我们几乎跟不上生产。我们必须扩展所有系统。

After our first year of sales in 2018, those next two years were crazy. We were barely keeping up with production. We had to scale all the systems.

看起来如何：强劲需求、满意度、效率案例研究

What it Looks Like: A Case Study on Strong Demand, Satisfaction, Efficiency in Action

Let's start again by tracing back to when Looker fit into Level 3, roughly from November 2014 to December 2015. By November 2014, the team had passed the $5M ARR mark.

让我们再次追溯到Looker符合Level 3的时候，大约从2014年11月到2015年12月。到2014年11月，团队已经超过了500万美元ARR的门槛。

In February 2015, the company raised a $30M Series B round, noting they'd grown to 110 employees, and increased revenue by 400%.

2015年2月，该公司筹集了3000万美元的B轮融资，指出他们已经增长到110名员工，收入增长了400%。

At this point, Looker had 250 customers, adding new customers such as Uber, Instacart, Plaid, and Etsy.

此时，Looker拥有250个客户，新增了Uber、Instacart、Plaid和Etsy等客户。

For our Level 3 case study, let's introduce GuardDog, a fictional incident management tool to help security teams seamlessly identify and address security issues.

对于我们的Level 3案例研究，让我们介绍GuardDog，一个虚构的事件管理工具，帮助安全团队无缝识别和解决安全问题。

They have 64 full-time employees and are planning to raise a $25M Series B. Their burn multiple is now 2.1, which is getting respectable.

他们有64名全职员工，正计划筹集2500万美元的B轮融资。他们的燃烧倍数现在是2.1，这正在变得令人尊敬。

Let's walk through each of the dimensions of product-market fit:

让我们逐一了解产品市场契合度的各个维度：

• Demand: GuardDog has 88 customers. With $10M in ARR and 14% sales conversion, they've got inbound and outbound channels that are working.

• 需求：GuardDog有88个客户。ARR为1000万美元，销售转化率为14%，他们有有效的inbound和outbound渠道。

• Satisfaction: They're at 108% net revenue retention, and 8% regretted churn per year. Quarterly user engagement has gone from 5.5 average users per customer up to nine.

• 满意度：他们的净收入保留率为108%，每年遗憾流失率为8%。季度用户参与度从每个客户平均5.5个用户上升到9个。

• Efficiency: GuardDog is getting into the respectable zone here. The team did a ton of work both on the infrastructure side, and the services side to improve to a 71% gross margin.

• 效率：GuardDog正在进入令人尊敬的区域。团队在基础设施方面和服务方面做了大量工作，将毛利率提高到71%。

• Demand source: 1+ scalable channels (you've cracked marketing and sales).

• 需求来源：1个以上可扩展渠道（你已经破解了营销和销售）。

最重要的是什么

What Matters Most

To reach the next level, now is the time that you have to get serious about efficient economics. CAC payback, LTV to CAC ratio — these are all the metrics that really start to matter.

要达到下一层级，现在是你必须认真对待高效经济的时候了。CAC回收期、LTV与CAC比率——这些都是真正开始重要的指标。

The difference between strong and extreme product-market fit is that you have to successfully stay on that growth curve and make the whole operation more efficient at the same time. It's a highwire act.

强劲和极致产品市场契合度之间的区别在于，你必须成功地保持在这条增长曲线上，同时使整个运营更高效。这是一种走钢丝的行为。

需要注意的警告信号

Yellow Flags to Watch Out for

• You've got a little bit of a leaky bucket: NRR below 90%, regretted churn greater than 10%.

• 你的桶有点漏：NRR低于90%，遗憾流失大于10%。

• Referrals and word of mouth have started to plateau.

• 推荐和口碑已经开始趋于平稳。

• Growth is slowing down. You grew 3X each of the prior two years, but you're struggling to hit 2X this year.

• 增长正在放缓。你在前两年每年增长3倍，但今年你正在努力达到2倍。

在设定季度目标时，不要偏离你的模型太远。在可以更快的地方走得更快，但不要采取让团队感觉像要溺水的粗暴方法。

When it comes to setting your targets for the quarter, don't stray too far from your model. Go faster where you can go faster, but don't take a blunt force approach that makes your team feel like they're drowning.
