【1. 财富定义】

Seek wealth, not money or status. Wealth is having assets that earn while you sleep. Money is how we transfer time and wealth. Status is your place in the social hierarchy.

寻求财富，而非金钱或地位。财富是指拥有能在你睡觉时仍为你赚钱的资产。金钱只是我们转移财富劳动成果的方式。地位是你在社会等级中的位置。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【2. 财富心态】

Understand that ethical wealth creation is possible. If you secretly despise wealth, it will elude you.

理解合乎道德的财富创造是可能的。如果你内心鄙视财富，财富就会远离你。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【3. 地位游戏】

Ignore people playing status games. They gain status by attacking people playing wealth creation games.

忽视那些玩地位游戏的人。他们通过攻击那些玩财富创造游戏的人来获取地位。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【4. 股权重要性】

You're not going to get rich renting out your time. You must own equity, a piece of a business, to gain your financial freedom.

你不会通过出租自己的时间变得富有。你必须拥有股权（企业的一部分）才能获得财务自由。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【5. 创造价值】

You will get rich by giving society what it wants, but does not yet know how to get, at scale.

通过给予社会它想要但还不知道如何获得的东西，你会变得富有。规模化提供这些产品。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【6. 长期游戏】

Pick an industry where you can play long-term games with long-term people.

挑选一个你可以与长期主义者玩长期游戏的行业。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【7. 互联网机遇】

The Internet has massively broadened the possible space of careers. Most people haven't figured this out yet.

互联网极大地拓宽了职业的可能空间。大多数人还没有意识到这一点。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【8. 复利效应】

Play iterated games. All the returns in life, whether in wealth, relationships, or knowledge, come from compound interest.

玩迭代游戏。生活中的所有回报，无论是财富、关系还是知识，都来自复利。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【9. 选择伙伴】

Pick business partners with high intelligence, high energy, and above all, high integrity.

选择具有高智商、高能量和高诚信的商业伙伴。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【10. 避免悲观者】

Don't partner with cynics and pessimists. Their beliefs are self-fulfilling.

不要与愤世嫉俗者和悲观者合作。他们的信仰是自我实现的。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【11. 核心技能】

Learn to sell. Learn to build. If you can do both, you will be unstoppable.

学习销售。学习建造。如果你两者都能做到，你将不可阻挡。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【12. 四大武器】

Arm yourself with specific knowledge, accountability, leverage, and judgment.

用特定知识、责任感、杠杆和判断力来武装自己。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【13. 特定知识】

Specific knowledge is knowledge that you cannot be trained for. If society can train you, it can train someone else and replace you.

特定知识是那种不能通过培训获得的知识。如果社会可以培训你，它也可以培训其他人来取代你。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【14. 发现特定知识】

Specific knowledge is found by pursuing your genuine curiosity and passion rather than whatever is hot right now.

特定知识是通过追求你真正的好奇心和热情而被发现的，而不是通过当下最热门的工作。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【15. 特定知识的特征】

Building specific knowledge will feel like play to you but will look like work to others.

建立特定知识对你来说会感觉像玩耍，但在别人看来像工作。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【16. 内在动机】

When you do things for their own sake, you create your best work. If you do it for money, you create the worst work.

当你为事情本身而做时，你会创造出最好的作品。如果你为了钱而做，你会创造出最糟糕的作品。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【17. 新杠杆】

The most powerful leverage is the one that humans have used forever: code and media. They are permissionless leverage.

最强大的杠杆是人类永远使用的：代码和媒体。它们是不需要许可的杠杆。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【18. 睡后收入】

Code and media are the leverage behind the newly rich. You can create software and media that works for you while you sleep.

代码和媒体是新富背后的杠杆。你可以创造软件和媒体，它们在你睡觉时为你工作。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【19. 机器人和AI】

An army of robots is freely available - it's just packed in data centers for heat and space efficiency. Use them.

一支机器人大军是免费可用的——它们只是被包装在数据中心里以提高热效率和空间效率。使用它们。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【20. 内容创作】

If you can't code, write books and blogs, record videos and podcasts. That's how you reach people at scale.

如果你不会编程，就写书和博客，录制视频和播客。这就是你规模化触达人群的方式。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【21. 判断力】

Leverage is a force multiplier for your judgment. Judgment requires experience but can be built faster by learning foundational skills.

杠杆是你判断力的力量倍增器。判断力需要经验，但可以通过学习基础技能更快地建立。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【22. 学什么】

There is no skill called 'business.' Avoid business magazines and business classes. Study microeconomics, game theory, psychology, persuasion, ethics, mathematics, and computers.

没有叫做'商业'的技能。避开商业杂志和商业课程。学习微观经济学、博弈论、心理学、说服术、伦理学、数学和计算机。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【23. 学习效率】

Reading is faster than listening. Doing is faster than watching.

阅读比听快。做比看快。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【24. 时间管理】

You should be too busy to 'do coffee,' while still keeping an uncluttered calendar.

你应该忙得没空'喝咖啡'，同时仍然保持日程表整洁。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【25. 终身学习】

The most important skill for getting rich is becoming a perpetual learner. You have to know how to learn anything you want to learn.

致富最重要的技能是成为一个终身学习者。你必须知道如何学习任何你想学习的东西。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

【26. 财富的本质】

When you're finally wealthy, you'll realize that it wasn't what you were seeking in the first place. But that's for another day.

当你最终变得富有时，你会发现那不是最初让你想要的东西。但那是另一天的故事。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
